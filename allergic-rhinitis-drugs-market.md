<p><p>The allergic rhinitis drugs market encompasses several key types, including antihistamines, intranasal corticosteroids, and immunotherapies. Antihistamines block the action of histamines, alleviating symptoms like sneezing and itching. Intranasal corticosteroids reduce inflammation in the nasal passages, providing effective relief from congestion and other symptoms. Immunotherapies, such as allergy shots or sublingual tablets, aim to desensitize the immune system to specific allergens over time. Together, these therapies offer a comprehensive approach to managing allergic rhinitis and enhancing patients' quality of life.</p></p>
