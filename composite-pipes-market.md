<p><p>Composite pipes are advanced piping systems made from a combination of materials, typically featuring a thermoplastic or thermoset resin reinforced with fibers such as glass, carbon, or aramid. Increasing investments in infrastructure development, coupled with the rise in exploration activities in the oil and gas sector, are propelling market expansion.
