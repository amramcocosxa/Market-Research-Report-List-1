<p><p>Metallurgy Additive Manufacturing for the aerospace market involves advanced techniques like Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses focused lasers to selectively melt metal powder, producing intricate and lightweight components with high precision. In contrast, EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal layers, enabling superior mechanical properties and faster production rates. Both methods are crucial for developing complex aerospace parts that meet stringent performance and safety standards while minimizing material waste.</p></p>
