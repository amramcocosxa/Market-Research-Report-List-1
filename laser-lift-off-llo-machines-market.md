<p><p>Laser Lift Off (LLO) machines utilize laser technology to separate thin films from substrates, primarily in semiconductor and display manufacturing. The market includes Excimer Laser Lift-Off machines, which use short-wavelength laser pulses for precise ablation, ideal for delicate applications. DPSS (Diode-Pumped Solid-State) Laser Lift-Off machines, on the other hand, employ solid-state lasers to achieve higher efficiency and longer operational lifespans. Both types enhance process control, minimize damage, and improve yield in the fabrication of advanced materials.</p></p>
