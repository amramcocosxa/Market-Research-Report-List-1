<p><p>The solar sputtering targets market is categorized into three main types: metal targets, alloy targets, and ceramic compound targets. Metal targets, primarily composed of pure metals, are favored for their high conductivity and efficiency in material deposition. Alloy targets combine two or more metals to enhance performance characteristics like durability and thermal resistance. Ceramic compound targets, made from non-metallic materials, offer unique chemical properties and are essential for specific applications in thin-film solar cell production, providing diverse options for manufacturers.</p></p>
