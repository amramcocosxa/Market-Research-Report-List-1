<p><p>The Peripheral I.V. Catheter market comprises two primary types: Short PIVC and Integrated/Closed PIVC. Short PIVCs are standard, flexible tubes used for temporary access to veins, facilitating quick infusion of fluids and medications. In contrast, Integrated/Closed PIVCs feature a more advanced design that reduces the risk of contamination and enhances patient safety by incorporating closed systems and built-in safety mechanisms. Both types cater to various clinical needs, ensuring efficient intravenous therapy across diverse healthcare settings.</p></p>
