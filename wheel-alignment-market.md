<p><ul><li>Thrust Alignment</li><li>Two-Wheel Alignment</li><li>Four-Wheel Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel alignment market is categorized into three main types: Thrust Alignment, Two-Wheel Alignment, and Four-Wheel Alignment. Thrust Alignment focuses on the front wheels and rear axle's orientation, ensuring the vehicle tracks straight. Two-Wheel Alignment adjusts only the front wheels, ideal for vehicles with a rear solid axle. Four-Wheel Alignment involves aligning all four wheels, which is crucial for vehicles with independent rear suspensions, promoting balanced handling and tire longevity. Each type addresses specific alignment needs based on vehicle design.</p></p>
