<p><p>The Hand Portable Digital Mobile Radio (DMR) market is categorized into high-frequency and low-frequency segments, based on the operating frequency of the devices. High-frequency DMRs typically operate between 30 MHz and 300 MHz, offering extended range and better signal penetration through obstacles, making them suitable for expansive rural and outdoor applications. In contrast, low-frequency DMRs, operating below 30 MHz, are primarily utilized in urban settings for localized communication, where shorter distances are common, but they excel in dense interference environments.</p></p>
