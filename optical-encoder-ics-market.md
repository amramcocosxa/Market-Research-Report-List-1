<p><strong>The Optical Encoder Ics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmissive Type</li><li>Reflective Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encoder IC market includes two main types: transmissive and reflective. Transmissive types operate by detecting changes in light passing through a coded disc, making them suitable for high-resolution applications. In contrast, reflective types utilize light reflected off a surface to detect position changes, generally offering simpler designs and cost-effectiveness. Both types are widely used in robotics, automation, and industrial applications, with varying requirements for precision, speed, and environmental conditions dictating their selection.</p></p>
