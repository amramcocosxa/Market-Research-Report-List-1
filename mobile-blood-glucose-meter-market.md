<p><strong>The Mobile Blood Glucose Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glucose Oxidase Method</li><li>Glucose Dehydrogenase Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile blood glucose meter market primarily consists of two main types: glucose oxidase and glucose dehydrogenase methods. The glucose oxidase method utilizes an enzyme that reacts with glucose to produce hydrogen peroxide, which is then measured to determine blood sugar levels. In contrast, the glucose dehydrogenase method employs a different enzyme, generating a measurable electrical signal in the presence of glucose. Both methods cater to the growing demand for convenient diabetes management, offering portability and user-friendly features.</p></p>
