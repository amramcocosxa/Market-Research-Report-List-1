<p><p>Mist oils are categorized primarily into two market types: non-strain type and anti-wear type. Non-strain type mist oils are designed for applications requiring minimal stress and friction reduction, enhancing machinery efficiency without significant load-bearing concerns. In contrast, anti-wear type mist oils are formulated to provide superior protection against wear and tear in high-stress environments. These oils contain additives that form protective films, minimizing metal-to-metal contact and extending equipment lifespan. Both types serve distinct roles in optimizing machinery performance.</p></p>
