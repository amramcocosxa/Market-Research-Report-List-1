<p><p>File migration software market is categorized into various types based on storage architecture. DFS (Distributed File System) facilitates seamless data access across multiple locations while ensuring redundancy. NAS (Network-Attached Storage) provides file-level storage accessible over a network, ideal for sharing among users. SAN (Storage Area Network) offers block-level storage optimized for high-speed performance and scalability, mainly for enterprise environments. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses various proprietary and cloud-based solutions tailored for specific organization needs and diverse environments.</p></p>
