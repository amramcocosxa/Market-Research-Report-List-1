<p><p>The cardiac care medical equipment market encompasses various types of devices critical for diagnosing and treating heart conditions. Cardiac ultrasound devices provide imaging for heart assessment. CRM devices, or cardiac rhythm management devices, regulate heart rhythms, and external defibrillators deliver shocks to restore normal heart function. Other equipment includes advanced monitoring tools and surgical instruments to enhance cardiac care.</p></p>
