<p><ul><li>Fillable Autoinjectors</li><li>Prefilled Autoinjectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The autoinjectors market comprises two main types: fillable autoinjectors and prefilled autoinjectors. Fillable autoinjectors allow users or healthcare professionals to fill the device with the desired medication before usage, offering flexibility in medication choice. In contrast, prefilled autoinjectors come pre-loaded with a specific drug, simplifying the administration process and enhancing convenience for patients. Both types are designed to facilitate self-administration and improve patient adherence, particularly for chronic conditions requiring regular injections.</p></p>
