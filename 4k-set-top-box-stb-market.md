<p><ul><li>Internet Protocol Television (IPTV)</li><li>Satellite Television</li><li>Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT)</li><li>Internet Television Set-top Box (OTT)</li><li>Hybrid Television Set-top Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 4K Set Top Box (STB) market encompasses various types: </p><p>1. Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) transmits content via internet protocols, enabling interactive features and on-demand services. </p><p>2. Satellite Television delivers content through satellite signals, requiring a dish for reception and often providing extensive channel choices. </p><p>3. Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT) broadcasts channels over terrestrial signals, accessible with an antenna.</p><p>4. Internet Television Set-top Box (OTT) streams content from the internet, offering flexibility without traditional cable. </p><p>5. Hybrid Television STBs combine multiple technologies, allowing seamless access to both broadcast and internet-based content.</p></p>
