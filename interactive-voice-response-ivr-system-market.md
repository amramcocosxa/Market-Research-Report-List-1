<p><p>The Interactive Voice Response (IVR) system market is primarily divided into two types: speech-based and touch-tone based. Speech-based IVR systems utilize natural language processing to understand and respond to user queries through spoken words, offering a more intuitive and flexible interaction. In contrast, touch-tone based systems rely on keypad inputs, where users navigate through menus by pressing numbers. Both types enhance customer experience by allowing automated call handling, but they cater to different user preferences and technological capabilities.</p></p>
