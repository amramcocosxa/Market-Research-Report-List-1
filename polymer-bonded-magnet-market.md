<p><strong>The Polymer Bonded Magnet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection magnet</li><li>Pressed magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polymer Bonded Magnet market encompasses two primary types: injection magnets and pressed magnets. Injection magnets are produced through a molding process where polymer and magnetic powders are injected into a cavity, resulting in complex shapes and high precision. Pressed magnets, on the other hand, involve compacting a mixture of magnetic materials and polymers under high pressure, followed by sintering, allowing for larger quantities and cost efficiency. Both types are essential for diverse applications in electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
