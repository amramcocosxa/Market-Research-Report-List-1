<p><p>The Overhead Catenary System (OCS) market is primarily divided into two types: Flexible and Rigid Overhead Catenary Systems. Flexible OCS utilizes flexible cables that can adapt to various movement dynamics, often used in lighter rail applications and areas requiring curvature. In contrast, Rigid OCS consists of fixed structures with solid support, providing stability and significant durability, commonly employed in heavy rail and freight applications. Both systems facilitate efficient electrification for rail transportation, enhancing operational reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
