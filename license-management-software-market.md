<p><p>License Management Software encompasses three key market types: hardware-based, software-based, and cloud-based enforcement. Hardware-based enforcement involves physical devices that manage and regulate software licenses, ensuring compliance through dedicated equipment. Software-based enforcement relies on installed applications that monitor and control license usage directly on devices, offering integration with existing systems. Cloud-based enforcement leverages internet connectivity, allowing for scalable, remote management of software licenses, providing real-time analytics and compliance monitoring without the need for on-premises infrastructure.</p></p>
