<p><strong>The AMOLED Electroluminescent Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emission Layer Materials</li><li>Common Layer Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMOLED electroluminescent materials market includes emission layer materials, which emit light when an electric current is applied, and common layer materials, which serve various functions such as electron transport, hole transport, and encapsulation. Emission layer materials typically consist of organic compounds or quantum dots, contributing to color production and brightness. Common layer materials encompass substances used to enhance device efficiency and performance, ensuring optimal charge injection and stability, thereby improving the overall functionality of AMOLED displays.</p></p>
