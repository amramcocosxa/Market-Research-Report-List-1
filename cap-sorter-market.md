<p><p>Cap Sorter market types include Rotary Sorters and Cross Belt Sorters, each designed for efficient sorting of caps in various production environments. Rotary Sorters use a rotating mechanism to guide caps into designated channels, ensuring high-speed sorting with minimal space requirements. Cross Belt Sorters utilize a series of belts running perpendicular to the flow of items, allowing for flexible sorting and easy integration into existing systems. Both types enhance operational efficiency and accuracy in cap sorting processes across industries.</p></p>
