<p><p>The fire extinguisher cylinders market includes several types, each designed for specific fire classes. Dry chemical extinguishers are versatile, effective against flammable liquids and electrical fires. Foam extinguishers are excellent for liquid fires, creating a barrier to smother flames. Carbon dioxide extinguishers are ideal for electrical fires, displacing oxygen to extinguish flames without residue. Water extinguishers effectively combat solid fires but shouldn't be used on flammable liquids. Other extinguisher types encompass special agents for unique fire risks, catering to diverse environments and applications.</p></p>
