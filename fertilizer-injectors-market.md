<p><p>The Fertilizer Injectors Market is primarily divided into Constant Pressure and Non-constant Pressure Types. Constant Pressure Injectors maintain a steady flow and pressure of fertilizers, ensuring uniform application regardless of varying system demands. They are ideal for consistent feeding in agricultural practices. In contrast, Non-constant Pressure Injectors adapt to fluctuating pressure levels, enabling efficient delivery based on system requirements. This flexibility allows for variable application rates, making them suitable for diverse farming scenarios and enhancing resource management.</p></p>
