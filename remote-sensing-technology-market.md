<p><ul><li>Active Remote Sensing</li><li>Passive Remote Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remote sensing technology market is categorized into active and passive remote sensing. Active remote sensing involves emitting signals, such as radar or laser, to measure the reflected energy from targets, providing detailed information regardless of sunlight conditions. In contrast, passive remote sensing receives natural energy reflected off surfaces, typically using satellites or sensors to capture data, primarily during daylight. Both types are vital for applications in agriculture, environmental monitoring, urban planning, and disaster management, enhancing decision-making and research.</p></p>
