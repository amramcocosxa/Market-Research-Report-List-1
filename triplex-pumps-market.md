<p><p>The Triplex Pumps market consists of Single-action and Double-action designs. Single-action Triplex Pumps utilize one stroke to pump fluid during each cycle, making them simple and efficient for low-pressure applications. In contrast, Double-action Triplex Pumps pump fluid during both strokes of the piston, offering improved efficiency and higher pressure capabilities. Both designs are prevalent in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and mining, providing reliable performance tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
