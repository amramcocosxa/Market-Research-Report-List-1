<p><p>Automotive financing services comprise various options for acquiring vehicles. A straightforward car loan involves borrowing a set amount to purchase a vehicle outright, repaid in installments. Hire purchase allows buyers to pay a deposit and then make monthly payments over time, after which ownership transfers. Personal contract hire, or car leasing, entails renting a vehicle for a specified period without ownership. Personal contract purchase combines leasing with an option to buy the car at the end, providing flexibility and lower monthly payments.</p></p>
