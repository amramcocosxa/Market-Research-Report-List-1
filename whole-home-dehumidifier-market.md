<p><p>The whole-home dehumidifier market is divided into two primary types: ducted and freestanding dehumidifiers. Ducted dehumidifiers integrate with the existing HVAC system, providing consistent humidity control throughout the entire home via ductwork. They are ideal for larger homes or those with existing duct systems. Freestanding dehumidifiers, on the other hand, are portable units that can be placed in specific areas to target localized moisture problems, making them suitable for smaller spaces or targeted dehumidification needs.</p></p>
