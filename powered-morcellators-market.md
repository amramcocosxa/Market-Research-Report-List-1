<p><p>Powered morcellators are surgical instruments used to remove tissue through small incisions during minimally invasive procedures, particularly in gynecological surgeries such as hysterectomies and myomectomies. These devices utilize rotational blades to cut tissue into smaller fragments, facilitating easier removal. The increasing preference for minimally invasive surgeries, coupled with advancements in surgical technology, significantly drives the demand for powered morcellators.</p><p>The Powered Morcellators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period. Regulatory bodies are also emphasizing the need for safety and efficacy in morcellation techniques, leading to innovations and improvements in product design. Furthermore, an aging population with a higher prevalence of gynecological conditions will contribute to increased demand, making powered morcellators a vital component in modern surgical practice.
