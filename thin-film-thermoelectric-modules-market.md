<p><p>Thin film thermoelectric modules are categorized into two primary market types: Deep Cooling Modules and General Purpose Modules. Deep Cooling Modules are designed for applications requiring efficient and precise temperature control, often used in scientific instruments and high-performance electronics. In contrast, General Purpose Modules cater to a broader range of applications, providing reliable cooling solutions for everyday devices like refrigerators and HVAC systems. Both types leverage thermoelectric technology, allowing for compact, solid-state cooling without moving parts, enhancing reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
