<p><strong>The Digital Impression Standalone Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Confocal Microscopic Imaging Scanners</li><li>Optical Wand Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital impression standalone scanners market features two primary types: confocal microscopic imaging scanners and optical wand scanners. Confocal microscopic imaging scanners utilize laser technology to capture high-resolution, detailed 3D images of dental structures, enhancing precision in impressions. Optical wand scanners, on the other hand, are handheld devices that capture images by projecting light onto surfaces, creating a real-time digital impression. Both technologies aim to improve the efficiency and accuracy of dental impressions, facilitating better treatment outcomes.</p></p>
