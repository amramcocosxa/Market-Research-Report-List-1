<p><p>The electric powertrain system market encompasses various components critical to electric and hybrid vehicles. Motors and generators convert electrical energy into motion and vice versa. Batteries store and supply energy, while power electronics controllers manage energy flow. Converters transform electrical currents to suitable formats, and transmission systems facilitate power transfer from the motor to the wheels. On-board chargers enable the battery to recharge while maintaining efficiency. Together, these components define the performance and efficiency of electric vehicle powertrains.</p></p>
