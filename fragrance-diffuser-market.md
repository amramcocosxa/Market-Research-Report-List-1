<p><p>The fragrance diffuser market comprises various types designed to disperse essential oils and scents. Ultrasonic diffusers utilize water and ultrasonic waves to create a mist, enhancing humidity and scent release. Evaporative diffusers use a fan to circulate air through absorbent material, promoting quicker evaporation of oils. Electric heat diffusers warm oils to facilitate scent dispersion. Other types may include reed diffusers, candle diffusers, or nebulizing diffusers, each offering unique methods for enhancing ambient fragrance in various settings.</p></p>
