<p><ul><li>Corrective Maintenance</li><li>Preventive Maintenance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor cleaning maintenance market encompasses two primary types: corrective maintenance and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance involves repairing or addressing floor issues after they occur, focusing on fixing damage and restoring cleanliness. In contrast, preventive maintenance aims to proactively maintain floors by scheduling regular cleaning and upkeep to avoid deterioration and prolong lifespan. Both approaches are essential for ensuring optimal floor conditions, enhancing aesthetics, and minimizing long-term costs associated with extensive repairs or replacements.</p></p>
